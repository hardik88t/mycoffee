Brewing Methods

Explore different brewing techniques to discover your perfect cup.

☕

Pour Over

Clean, bright flavors with full control over extraction. Perfect for highlighting single origin characteristics.

Ratio: 1:15
Grind: Medium-Fine
Time: 3-4 minutes
Water Temp: 195-205°F
🫖

French Press

Full-bodied, rich coffee with oils and sediment. Simple immersion brewing method.

Ratio: 1:12
Grind: Coarse
Time: 4 minutes
Water Temp: 200°F
☕

Espresso

Concentrated, intense coffee with rich crema. Foundation for milk-based drinks.

Ratio: 1:2
Grind: Fine
Time: 25-30 seconds
Pressure: 9 bars
🔄

AeroPress

Versatile brewing method combining immersion and pressure for clean, smooth coffee.

Ratio: 1:14
Grind: Medium-Fine
Time: 1-2 minutes
Water Temp: 175-185°F
🧊

Cold Brew

Smooth, low-acid coffee brewed with cold water over extended time. Perfect for hot days.

Ratio: 1:8
Grind: Coarse
Time: 12-24 hours
Water Temp: Room Temp
⏳

Chemex

Clean, crisp coffee with bright flavors. Thick filters remove oils and sediment.

Ratio: 1:16
Grind: Medium-Coarse
Time: 4-6 minutes
Water Temp: 200°F
