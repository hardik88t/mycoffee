Our Coffee Story

A journey of passion, dedication, and the relentless pursuit of the perfect cup. Discover the people and values behind My Coffee.

How It All Began

My Coffee started in 2014 with a simple dream: to share exceptional coffee with our community. What began as a small operation in a converted garage has grown into a beloved roastery, but our commitment to quality and craftsmanship remains unchanged.

Our founder, Sarah Martinez, discovered her passion for coffee during travels through Central America, where she learned about the intricate process from bean to cup. Inspired by the dedication of coffee farmers and the complexity of flavors, she returned home with a mission to bring that same level of care to every roast.

Today, we continue to honor those relationships with farmers, roast in small batches, and share our knowledge with fellow coffee enthusiasts. Every cup tells a story of tradition, innovation, and the pursuit of excellence.
